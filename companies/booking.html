Booking Holdings on AI

Leveraging generative AI to realize its long-standing "Connected Trip" vision while preparing for agentic AI to reshape how travelers discover and book travel.

What the CEO Said

"It's an extraordinary time to be in the travel industry, with generative AI accelerating the pace of innovation and evolving how we deliver our mission, which is: to make it easier for everyone to experience the world."

Glenn Fogel, CEO, Q4 2025

"Agentic models will change the way some customers discover and use our platform. Working with these models can generate unique value with loyalty benefits and rewards, high quality customer service, and an easy and trusted payment process. It's critical to deliver value and continue to be a trusted platform our customers choose to make bookings with us."

Glenn Fogel, CEO, Q4 2025

"In everything we do, we leverage our global breadth, proprietary data, deep supplier integrations, decades of travel industry experience, and talented teams to accelerate the realization of AI's value across our business and for our customers."

Glenn Fogel, CEO, Q4 2025

"Ultimately, our goal is to leverage AI to make it easier for everyone to experience the world."

Glenn Fogel, CEO, Q4 2025

The Numbers

  • Connected trip transactions grew in the high 20% range annually, now making up a low double-digit percentage of Booking.com total transactions
  • 68 million airline tickets booked across platforms, up 37% YoY
  • Attraction tickets grew nearly 80% YoY (off a small base)
  • AI-powered customer service delivered YoY decrease in customer service costs while growing double-digit on gross bookings and revenue
  • AI integration showing better conversion and lower cancellation rates in early testing
  • 20% rise in travelers booking multiple trip components

AI Products & Initiatives

  • AI Trip Planner — Fully integrated into the Booking.com mobile app in 10+ countries, uses generative AI to build complex itineraries based on conversational prompts
  • Connected Trip Platform — AI-powered cross-vertical booking experience spanning hotels, flights, attractions, and ground transport
  • AI Customer Service — Generative AI integrated into customer service processes, reducing costs while maintaining quality
  • Agentic AI Preparation — Building capabilities to work with AI agent models that will change how customers discover and interact with the platform
  • 2026 Focus — Further connecting agentic capabilities for a more unified and personalized experience
