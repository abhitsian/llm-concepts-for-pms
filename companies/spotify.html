SPOT

Spotify on AI

Transform from a passive music player into "the world's most intelligent, agentic media service that you can literally talk to," powered by a proprietary taste graph no one else is building.

What the CEO Said

"We consider ourselves the R&D department for the music industry. Our job is to understand new technologies quickly and capture their potential. The entire industry stands to benefit from this paradigm shift but we believe those who embrace this change and move fast, will benefit the most."

Gustav Soderstrom, Co-CEO, Q4 2025

"The next evolution of Spotify [is] delivering the world's most intelligent, agentic media platform, one that you can literally talk to, that fully understands each individual listener and puts them in the driver's seat. It's about moving from a passive experience to an interactive one."

Gustav Soderstrom, Co-CEO, Q4 2025

"You can't just have an LLM commoditise it as a fact, the way you can commoditise Wikipedia. You actually need to have many, many hundreds of millions of listeners across the world constantly telling you what it means for that specific person. This is the data set we are building right now, and no one else is really building it."

Gustav Soderstrom, Co-CEO, Q4 2025

"The best developers we had, they actually say that they have not written a single line of code since December."

Gustav Soderstrom, Co-CEO, Q4 2025

The Numbers

  • AI DJ used by ~90 million subscribers, driving over 4 billion hours of listening time
  • Record 38 million net new monthly active users added in Q4 2025
  • Top engineers have not manually written code since December 2025, relying entirely on internal AI coding system built on Claude Code
  • Building a first-of-its-kind dataset mapping language to music, podcasts, and audiobooks at scale
  • Q4 2025 results beat forecasts; record free cash flow for full year 2025

AI Products & Initiatives

  • AI DJ -- interactive AI-hosted radio experience; 90M+ subscribers engaged
  • Prompted Playlist -- Premium users describe what they want to hear in natural language; AI generates a playlist
  • Taste Graph -- proprietary dataset of listener preferences across hundreds of millions of users; positioned as an un-commoditizable moat
  • Agentic media platform -- vision for a service users can literally talk to, moving from passive to interactive
  • AI-powered development -- internal AI coding system (built on Claude Code) has transformed engineering workflows; developers managing code from mobile devices
