Twilio on AI

Transforming from a communications API provider into the "customer experience layer of the internet" -- becoming essential AI infrastructure for brands building voice AI agents and personalized, memory-driven customer interactions.

What the CEO Said

"Twilio's potential is to be the customer experience layer of the internet."

Khozema Shipchandler, CEO (Q4 2025, February 2026)

"We remain focused on our vision of creating amazing experiences for brands, and are furiously building new and exciting capabilities that capitalize on all that AI has to offer. These innovations will allow Twilio to deliver memory-driven orchestration and agentic interactions that inspire engagement and trust. This is why Twilio is an essential infrastructure layer for every company's tech stack."

Khozema Shipchandler, CEO (Q4 2025, February 2026)

"2025 was a terrific year. We made tremendous progress against our goals, exceeded our targets for the year, and are well positioned to sustain this momentum into 2026 with our robust innovation roadmap."

Khozema Shipchandler, CEO (Q4 2025, February 2026)

The Numbers

  • Voice AI revenue growth accelerated above 60% year-over-year in Q4 2025
  • Branded calling revenue grew roughly 6x year-over-year in Q4
  • Full year 2025: $5.1B in revenue (14% YoY growth), $924M non-GAAP operating income, $945M free cash flow
  • Q4 2025: $1.4B revenue, $256M non-GAAP operating income
  • Conversation Relay (AI agent toolkit) seeing rapid adoption in healthcare and other verticals

AI Products & Initiatives

  • Conversation Relay -- framework for building voice AI agents on Twilio infrastructure
  • Branded Calling -- AI-verified caller identity to improve answer rates
  • Conversational Intelligence -- AI-powered call analysis
  • CustomerAI -- AI layer connecting customer data with communication channels
  • Memory-driven orchestration for agentic customer interactions
