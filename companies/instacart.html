Instacart on AI

Using AI "across the board" -- from 80%+ AI-assisted code deployment to AI-powered shopping assistants and ad personalization -- to deepen retailer relationships and build a full-stack grocery commerce platform.

What the CEO Said

"It is fair to say that we are using AI across the board to accelerate and improve all aspects of our business."

Instacart management (Q2 2025 earnings call, August 2025)

"We're deepening customer and retailer relationships, expanding our ads ecosystem, and launching innovative AI-powered tools across all aspects of our business -- all while driving profitable growth."

Chris Rogers, CEO (Q4 2025 earnings call, February 2026)

"Over 80% of code deployed in Q2 was AI-assisted, with a 30% increase in code merges per engineer year over year."

Instacart management (Q2 2025 earnings call, August 2025)

The Numbers

  • 80%+ of deployed code was AI-assisted (Q2 2025)
  • 30% increase in code merges per engineer year-over-year
  • Q2 2025: 17% YoY order growth, 11% YoY GTV growth
  • Record GTV and order growth rates in Q4 2025; shares climbed 14% after results
  • AI also automates code reviews and boosts sales and legal team efficiency
  • Former CEO Fidji Simo left for OpenAI in mid-2025, replaced by Chris Rogers

AI Products & Initiatives

  • Cart Assistant -- white-label AI shopping assistant for retail partners
  • SmartShot technology -- AI-powered product recognition
  • CaperCart and StoreView -- physical AI capabilities in stores, including smart carts and store mapping
  • AI-enhanced ad technology -- ranking, relevance, and personalization for sponsored products
  • Agentic shopping experiences -- beta testing, broader rollout planned by end Q1 2026
