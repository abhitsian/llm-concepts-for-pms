SNAP

Snap Inc. on AI

Distribute AI agents through Snapchat's chat interface, combining its own My AI chatbot with strategic partnerships (Perplexity) and using AR as the ideal AI interface.

What the CEO Said

"We have a really unique opportunity ahead to help distribute AI agents through our chat interface."

Evan Spiegel, CEO, Q3 2025

"Augmented reality is the ideal interface for AI."

Evan Spiegel, CEO, Q1 2025

"Something like 40% of new code at Snap is AI-generated."

Evan Spiegel, CEO, Q4 2025

"We've made a ton of headway with trust and safety and customer service in terms of automating those workflows... there's a lot of opportunity for the sales workflow as well to empower our sales team."

Evan Spiegel, CEO, Q4 2025

The Numbers

  • My AI daily active users grew 55%+ year-over-year in the U.S. (Q1 2025)
  • Snapchat surpassed 900 million monthly active users (Q1 2025); 477 million DAU in Q3 2025, up 8% YoY
  • $400 million one-year deal with Perplexity AI for integration into Snapchat chat (announced Q3 2025)
  • Q3 2025 revenue of $1.51 billion, beating estimates; Q1 2025 revenue of $1.36 billion, up 14% YoY
  • 40% of new code at Snap is AI-generated (Q4 2025)

AI Products & Initiatives

  • My AI chatbot -- multimodal AI assistant in Snapchat, seeing strong engagement growth
  • Perplexity AI integration -- AI-powered answer engine with "default placement in chat inbox," designed to drive Perplexity subscriptions
  • AI-powered trust & safety -- automating content moderation and customer service workflows
  • AI sales tools -- empowering sales team with AI-assisted workflows
  • AR + AI convergence -- positioning augmented reality as the ideal future interface for AI
