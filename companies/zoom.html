ZM

Zoom on AI

Evolving from a video conferencing tool into an "AI-first system of action" -- bundling AI Companion for free to drive adoption, then monetizing with Custom AI Companion for enterprise workflow automation.

What the CEO Said

"We're evolving Zoom into an AI-first system of action."

Eric Yuan, CEO (Q4 FY2025, February 2025)

"AI, AI, and AI."

Eric Yuan, CEO, when asked about investment priorities (Q4 FY2025, February 2025)

"Each of our top 10 deals in the quarter included paid AI; 7 represented competitive displacements of other CCaaS vendors."

Eric Yuan, CEO (Q4 FY2025, February 2025)

The Numbers

  • AI Companion monthly active users grew 68% quarter-over-quarter in Q4 FY2025
  • AI Companion monthly active users more than tripled year-over-year; side panel users more than doubled QoQ
  • Custom AI Companion priced at ~$12/user/month (launched April 2025)
  • Q4 FY2025 revenue: $1.247B, up 5.3% YoY; full year $4.869B, up 4.4% YoY
  • CEO Eric Yuan used his own AI avatar to deliver opening comments on earnings calls

AI Products & Initiatives

  • AI Companion -- free AI assistant for meeting summaries, chat, email, and docs, included at no cost for all paid tiers
  • Custom AI Companion -- paid add-on with custom agents, workflow builders, third-party integrations, and enterprise knowledge retrieval
  • AI-powered Contact Center (CCaaS) with AI-driven customer service
  • AI avatars for asynchronous video communication
  • Yuan's vision: AI will eventually shorten the workweek
