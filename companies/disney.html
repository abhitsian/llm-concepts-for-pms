DIS

Walt Disney on AI

Invest $1 billion in OpenAI and license 200+ iconic characters to Sora, betting that "creativity is the new productivity" and positioning Disney+ as an AI-powered interactive entertainment platform.

What the CEO Said

"AI is going to give us the ability to provide users of Disney+ with a much more engaged experience, including the ability for them to create user-generated content and to consume user-generated content, mostly short form, from others."

Bob Iger, CEO, Q4 FY2025 Earnings Call

"We've always viewed technological advances as opportunity, not threat. It's going to happen regardless, and we'd rather participate in the rather dramatic growth, rather than just watching it happen and essentially being disrupted by it."

Bob Iger, CEO, December 2025

"Creativity is the new productivity."

Bob Iger, CEO, December 2025 (on the $1B OpenAI investment)

"[AI has] a number of possible advantages or opportunities. One is as a tool to help the creative process -- creativity. Another is productivity, being more efficient. The third is connectivity -- creating a more intimate relationship with the consumer."

Bob Iger, CEO, Q4 FY2025

The Numbers

  • $1 billion equity investment in OpenAI (announced December 2025)
  • 200+ Disney characters (Mickey Mouse, Darth Vader, Cinderella, Iron Man, Ariel, etc.) licensed to Sora under a three-year agreement
  • Streaming revenue increased 13% year-over-year
  • Disney+ described as undergoing "the biggest and most significant changes from a product and technology perspective since we launched the service in 2019"

AI Products & Initiatives

  • OpenAI/Sora character licensing -- curated 30-second AI-generated videos featuring Disney characters on Sora; no human voices or faces; designed as a roadmap for interactive short-form content
  • Disney+ AI-powered UGC -- enabling subscribers to create and consume AI-generated short-form content using Disney IP
  • AI in creative production -- tools to assist the creative process (pre-visualization, set design, visual effects)
  • AI-driven personalization -- improving content discovery and subscriber engagement on Disney+
  • Game-like interactive features -- exploring gamification and AI-driven interactive experiences on Disney+
