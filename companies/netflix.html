NFLX

Netflix on AI

Go "all in" on generative AI as a creator tool, discovery engine, and ad platform enhancer -- while firmly insisting AI will never replace human storytelling.

What the CEO Said

"We're confident that AI is going to help us and help our creative partners tell stories better, faster and in new ways -- we're all in on that."

Ted Sarandos, Co-CEO, Q3 2025

"AI can give creatives better tools to enhance their overall TV and movie experience for our members, but it doesn't automatically make you a great storyteller if you're not."

Ted Sarandos, Co-CEO, Q3 2025

"We're not worried about AI replacing creativity, but we're very excited about AI creating tools to help creativity."

Ted Sarandos, Co-CEO, Q3 2025

"We've had a long history of developing machine learning and AI solutions. We've got a deep technology DNA, significant data assets, scaled consumer products and scaled business processes -- all of that enables us to leverage new technical capabilities as they come online."

Greg Peters, Co-CEO, Q3 2025

"We think generative AI has tremendous potential to improve our recommendations and discovery systems even further. We want to make it even easier for people to find an amazing story that's just perfect for them in that moment."

Greg Peters, Co-CEO, Q3 2025

The Numbers

  • Q3 2025 revenue of $11.51 billion, up 17% year-over-year
  • FY2025 projected revenue of $45.1 billion (16% growth) with 29% operating margin
  • Q3 operating income of $3.25 billion, up 12% YoY
  • GenAI conversational search in beta testing (using OpenAI's ChatGPT)

AI Products & Initiatives

  • AI conversational search -- beta testing natural-language discovery ("I want something funny and upbeat") powered by ChatGPT
  • GenAI in production -- used for de-aging in Happy Gilmore 2 (flashback sequence), pre-visualization and set design in Billionaires' Bunker
  • AI-powered content localization -- automating promotional material translation and adaptation
  • AI ad formats -- experimenting with new generative ad experiences
  • Recommendation engine evolution -- decade-plus ML foundation being enhanced with generative AI capabilities
