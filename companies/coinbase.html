The Numbers

  • Base (L2 network) set a new transaction all-time high with AI agents adopting stablecoin wallets (Q4 2025)
  • USDC market cap reached approximately $75 billion
  • 2024 total revenue more than doubled to $6.6 billion, with $3.3B in adjusted EBITDA
  • Subscription services revenue up 64% YoY to $2.3 billion (driven by USDC, staking, Coinbase One)

AI Products & Initiatives

  • AgentKit — Open-source toolkit (launched Nov 2024) that lets any AI agent embed a crypto wallet and conduct stablecoin payments, framework-agnostic and wallet-agnostic
  • Agentic Wallets — Purpose-built crypto wallets for autonomous AI agents, enabling gasless transactions via Smart Wallet API
  • Base as "Onchain Home for AI" — Positioning Base L2 network as the primary blockchain for AI agent transactions
  • Coinbase Developer Platform (CDP) — Full developer suite enabling AI-crypto integration, used by thousands of developers in hackathons and major integrations
