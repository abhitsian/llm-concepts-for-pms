AAPL

Apple on AI

Privacy-first, on-device AI through Apple Intelligence integrated across the entire product ecosystem -- using Apple silicon as the competitive moat and leveraging the installed base as a distribution advantage.

What the CEO Said

"We see AI as one of the most profound technologies of our lifetime. We are embedding it across our devices and platforms and across the company. We are also significantly growing our investments."

Tim Cook, CEO, Q3 FY2025

"Our focus, from an AI point of view, is on putting AI features across the platform that are deeply personal, private and seamlessly integrated."

Tim Cook, CEO, FY2025

"Apple has always been about taking the most advanced technologies and making them easy to use and accessible for everyone, and that's at the heart of our AI strategy."

Tim Cook, CEO, FY2025

"Apple Intelligence is a factor, and we're very bullish on it becoming a greater factor."

Tim Cook, CEO, Q1 FY2025

"We're very open to M&A that accelerates our roadmap."

Tim Cook, CEO, Q4 FY2025

The Numbers

  • FY2025 total revenue: $416B; Q4 FY2025 revenue a record $102.5B (+7.9% YoY)
  • Q3 FY2025 revenue: $94B (+10% YoY), net profit $23.4B
  • Services revenue climbed to $28.75B in Q4, up 15%+ YoY, with 75.3% margins
  • Services overtook iPhone as the largest profit contributor for the first time: 42% vs 41%
  • Capital expenditure rose 34.5% to $12.7B, reflecting AI infrastructure investment
  • Markets where Apple Intelligence was rolled out outperformed markets where it was not
  • Massive installed base of older iPhones (iPhone 12/13 users) entering upgrade window
  • Next-generation AI-powered Siri on track for 2026 launch

AI Products & Initiatives

  • Apple Intelligence -- on-device AI features across iOS, iPadOS, macOS
  • Private Cloud Compute -- privacy-preserving cloud AI architecture
  • Visual Intelligence -- real-world object recognition and contextual information
  • Live Translation -- real-time cross-language communication
  • Writing Tools -- summarization, rewriting, proofreading across system
  • OpenAI/ChatGPT integration into Siri and system-wide features
  • Next-generation Siri -- deeply personalized, agentic, expected 2026
  • Apple silicon optimized for on-device AI inference (A-series, M-series chips)
