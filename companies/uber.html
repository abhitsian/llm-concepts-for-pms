UBER

Uber on AI

Embedding generative AI across the entire platform -- from ride matching and demand prediction to driver/consumer AI assistants -- while positioning as the indispensable aggregation layer for autonomous vehicles at scale.

What the CEO Said

"I'm even more convinced that AVs will unlock a multi-trillion dollar opportunity. Autonomy fundamentally amplifies the strengths of our existing platform."

Dara Khosrowshahi, CEO (Q4 2025, February 2026)

"We are embedding intelligence across Uber to enhance productivity, optimize our operations and deliver more personalized consumer experiences."

Dara Khosrowshahi, CEO (Q4 2025, February 2026)

"Autonomous vehicles are likely to remain a very small portion of the rideshare category for many years to come."

Dara Khosrowshahi, CEO (Q4 2025, February 2026)

"You can imagine the majority of our trips being fulfilled by robots of some kind. Probably not 10 years from now, but you go 15 to 20 years from now."

Dara Khosrowshahi, CEO (February 2026)

The Numbers

  • Q4 2025 gross bookings: $54B, up 22% YoY; revenue: $14B, up 20% YoY
  • 200M+ monthly active users completing 40M+ trips per day
  • AV trips per vehicle per day ~30% higher on Uber vs. standalone AV platforms
  • AV rides live in 7 cities, expanding to 15 by end of 2025/2026
  • In AV-enabled cities (Atlanta, Austin), overall trip growth "significantly accelerated" -- including for human drivers
  • Piloting first-party AI agents: AI assistant for drivers, consumer-facing AI agents
  • ChatGPT integration for restaurant/service discovery

AI Products & Initiatives

  • AI-powered ride matching and demand prediction optimization
  • First-party AI assistants for drivers and consumers
  • ChatGPT integration -- discover services and restaurants before checkout
  • Autonomous vehicle aggregation platform (partnerships with Waymo, others)
  • AI-driven "platform for work" -- offering AI gigs to drivers and PhDs alike
