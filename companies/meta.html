Meta Platforms on AI

Build personal super-intelligence for billions through massive infrastructure investment, open-source Llama models, and deep AI integration across its 3.3B-user app ecosystem.

What the CEO Said

"We're seeing the returns... I think this is the right strategy to aggressively front-load building capacity."

Mark Zuckerberg, CEO, Q3 2025

"We will continue to invest very significantly in infrastructure to train leading models and deliver personal super intelligence to billions of people and businesses around the world."

Mark Zuckerberg, CEO, Q4 2025

"I think this very well could be the year when Llama and open source become the most advanced and widely used AI models."

Mark Zuckerberg, CEO, Q4 2024 (January 2025 call)

"We can't risk being constrained to what others in the ecosystem are building. Controlling a model allows you to help shape the future of these products."

Mark Zuckerberg, CEO, Q3 2025

"The focus for this year is deepening the experience and making Meta AI the leading personal AI with an emphasis on personalization, voice conversations and entertainment."

Mark Zuckerberg, CEO, Q1 2025

The Numbers

  • Meta AI reached 1 billion monthly active users (announced May 2025), doubling from 500M in September 2024 -- fastest AI platform growth in history
  • Llama models surpassed 1 billion cumulative downloads (March 2025), up ~53% from 650M in December 2024
  • FY2025 CapEx guidance raised to $70-72 billion; FY2026 CapEx guidance of $115-135 billion (nearly 2x year-over-year)
  • Q3 2025 revenue of $51.24 billion, up 26% year-over-year; Q4 revenue growth of 24% YoY
  • 3.35 billion daily active users across Family of Apps (Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp, Messenger)

AI Products & Initiatives

  • Meta AI Assistant -- integrated across Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp, Messenger; standalone app launched April 2025
  • Llama open-source models -- Llama 4 Behemoth positioned as "highest performing base model in the world"; used in production by Spotify, AT&T, DoorDash, and others
  • AI-powered ad targeting and recommendations -- driving the core revenue engine with improved ad efficiency
  • AI content creation tools -- for creators across Instagram and Facebook
  • Monetization roadmap -- exploring paid recommendations and subscription tiers for heavier AI compute usage
