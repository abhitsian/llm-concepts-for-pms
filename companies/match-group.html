MTCH

Match Group on AI

New CEO betting that AI will create the same "Cambrian explosion" for dating that mobile did a decade ago, with $60M earmarked for AI-powered matching at Tinder.

What the CEO Said

"This Cambrian explosion in AI is going to allow Match Group to have the same kind of business inflection that the shift from desktop to mobile created for our category and other categories around 10 years ago — when mobile exceeded desktop."

Spencer Rascoff, CEO, Q4 2024

"We believe AI is a core enabler of how we improve relevance and matching."

Spencer Rascoff, CEO, 2025

"[The AI-driven matching feature will give users] something other than swiping."

Match Group CFO, 2025

The Numbers

  • $60 million budget allocated for AI and product rollouts at Tinder
  • AI initiatives expected to cause a 1.5 percentage point headwind to near-term monetization
  • Hinge delivered strong 27% revenue growth in Q3 2025, while Tinder saw a 3% decline — underscoring urgency of AI-driven transformation
  • Project Aurora (Australia test market): Tinder "sparks" improved from down 14% YoY in Dec 2024 to down ~8% YoY in Dec 2025

AI Products & Initiatives

  • AI-Driven Recommendation Algorithms — New algorithms that affect the order of profiles shown, particularly to women users
  • AI-Powered Discovery & Matching — Rolling out features designed to replace the traditional "swipe" mechanic with AI-curated experiences
  • Project Aurora — Test market in Australia combining multiple high-conviction AI product changes, serving as a learning engine for global rollout
  • CEO Transition — Spencer Rascoff (Zillow co-founder) appointed CEO in Feb 2025, bringing technology-first perspective to dating
