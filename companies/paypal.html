PayPal on AI

Positioning as the default payments layer for the agentic AI economy while using AI internally to personalize checkout and fight fraud.

Alex Chriss, CEO, Q4 2024

"We also are leveraging AI to personalize the commerce journey, working with our merchants to be able to understand and create this really magical experience for consumers."

Alex Chriss, CEO, Q4 2024

Alex Chriss, CEO, Q3 2025

Alex Chriss, CEO, Q3 2025

The Numbers

  • Upgraded checkout experiences reduce latency by more than 40% and drive more than 100 basis points of conversion lift on average
  • 26 billion transactions on PayPal's platform in 2024, providing a massive AI training dataset
  • Q3 2025: $8.42B revenue, raised full-year outlook; stock jumped 8.5% on earnings beat

AI Products & Initiatives

  • Agentic Commerce Services — Helps merchants sell through multiple AI platforms including Google, OpenAI, and Perplexity
  • OpenAI/ChatGPT Partnership — PayPal branded checkout integrated into ChatGPT via Agentic Commerce Protocol (ACP), enabling instant checkout for hundreds of millions of ChatGPT users
  • Fastlane — One-click guest checkout powered by AI
  • AI-Powered Scam Alerts — Dynamic scam detection for PayPal and Venmo Friends & Family payments
  • AI Personalization Engine — Using 26B transactions of data to personalize the commerce experience for consumers

The AI Strategy

PayPal is positioning as the payment infrastructure for agentic commerce. When AI agents shop on your behalf, someone needs to process the payment — securely, with fraud protection, across currencies. The ChatGPT integration (branded checkout via Agentic Commerce Protocol) is the first proof point: AI agents routing real transactions through PayPal.

The data advantage is massive: 26 billion annual transactions create a dataset for fraud detection, personalization, and purchase behavior understanding that no startup can replicate. This is a data moat built over 25 years of transaction history — the kind of deep, exclusive, continuously-refreshed data that AI makes more valuable, not less.

The “40% latency reduction, 100 basis points conversion lift” from upgraded checkout experiences shows the near-term AI value. But the long-term bet is on agentic commerce — Chriss’s “the upside is a multiple of where we are today” signals a belief that AI-mediated transactions will dramatically expand the total volume flowing through payment rails.

The Counterintuitive Insight

AI agents need payment rails, and PayPal is building them before the agents fully arrive. When ChatGPT recommends a product and you say “buy it,” someone needs to process the payment. Credit cards require human input. Bank transfers require human identity. But PayPal’s Agentic Commerce Protocol lets AI agents complete purchases programmatically. PayPal is betting that AI shopping agents will route more transactions through trusted platforms, not fewer.
