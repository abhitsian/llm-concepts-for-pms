Costco on AI

Taking a characteristically practical, member-focused approach to AI -- deploying it first in pharmacy inventory and gas station management where it delivers tangible cost savings, rather than making splashy AI announcements.

What the CEO Said

"AI is also being interwoven into our business where we believe it can strengthen our model. We're approaching it in a very Costco way -- practical, member-focused and grounded in tangible business value."

Ron Vachris, CEO (Q1 FY2026 earnings call, December 2025)

"An early use case has involved integrating AI into our pharmacy inventory system. This system now compares prescription drug pricing across vendors and autonomously and predictively reorders inventory, improving our in-stocks to more than 98%."

Ron Vachris, CEO (Q1 FY2026 earnings call, December 2025)

"This change has played an important role in helping us achieve mid-teen growth in pharmacy scripts filled and has improved margins while lowering prices to our members."

Ron Vachris, CEO (Q1 FY2026 earnings call, December 2025)

The Numbers

  • AI-powered pharmacy inventory raised in-stocks above 98%
  • Mid-teen growth in pharmacy scripts filled, attributed partly to AI inventory system
  • Improved margins while simultaneously lowering member prices (AI-driven vendor price comparison)
  • AI being deployed in gas station business for inventory management
  • Pre-scan checkout technology increased checkout speeds by up to 20% at adopting locations

AI Products & Initiatives

  • AI pharmacy inventory system -- autonomous pricing comparison and predictive reordering
  • AI gas station inventory management (in deployment)
  • Pre-scan checkout technology -- 20% faster checkout
  • Overall strategy described as "early innings" -- exploring demand forecasting and inventory optimization
  • No major consumer-facing AI product announcements (consistent with Costco's operational focus)
