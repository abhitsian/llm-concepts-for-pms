The Numbers

  • AWS annual revenue run rate: $123B to $142B across 2025
  • AWS revenue growth: 24% in recent quarter -- strongest growth rate in 13 quarters
  • AWS AI business: multi-billion dollar run rate, triple-digit YoY growth
  • AWS AI growing 3x faster at this stage than AWS itself grew at the same stage
  • Amazon Bedrock: multi-billion dollar annualized run rate; customer spend up 60% QoQ
  • Trainium custom chips: ~30-40% better price-performance than competing GPUs
  • Trainium2 reached multi-billion dollar revenue run rate
  • 2025 CapEx planned: ~$100B; 2026 CapEx expected: ~$200B (majority for AWS)
  • Trainium3 (next-gen chip): 4x faster than Trainium2

AI Products & Initiatives

  • Amazon Bedrock -- managed model hosting (Anthropic Claude, Meta Llama, Mistral, Amazon Titan)
  • Trainium custom AI chips -- Trainium2 in production, Trainium3 announced
  • Amazon SageMaker -- ML platform for training and deployment
  • Amazon Q -- AI assistant for developers and business users
  • Alexa+ -- LLM-powered next-gen voice assistant
  • Rufus -- AI shopping assistant
  • AI-powered logistics optimization and robotics
  • Strategic partnership with Anthropic -- up to $4B investment; Anthropic runs on AWS/Trainium
